Who Are We

STOMP Out Bullying is the most prominent anti-bullying non-profit organization for children and teenagers in the United States. Not only has STOMP Out Bullying won numerous awards for bullying prevention, but also the prevention of cyberbullying. We are commited to reduce bullying and cyberbullying based on racism, hatred, homophobia, violence, and beliefs. Raising awareness through our various campaigns, we hope to stomp out bullying.

Rise to the Challenge

Blue Shirt Day

The Blue Shirt Day World Day of Bullying Prevention is the first Monday of each October. Wear a blue shirt to signify the importance of bullying prevention. Spread the word at your schools and community to get involved.

No Matter

Celebrate the fact that we are all people in teh No Matter event. No one deserves to get bullied for any reason. Instead, let's honor our differences and embrace our similarities through respect.

National Bullying Prevention Awareness Month

The whole month of October is known as National Bullying Prevention Awareness Month. The goal is to encourage communities to work together to stop bullying and to bring awareness to children and teenagers of all ages.
